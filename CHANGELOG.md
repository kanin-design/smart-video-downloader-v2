# Smart Video Downloader V2 Changelog

## [Initial Version] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

- Download videos from YouTube, Vimeo, Twitter/X, TikTok, Instagram, and 1000+ other sites via yt-dlp
- Download Manager: paste a URL, pick a format, track every download in the background
- Download Video: detect the video in your active browser tab and preview metadata before downloading
- Quick Download: instant global hotkey that downloads the active browser tab's video headlessly, no UI required
- Cookie Settings: extract cookies from your browser to download age-restricted, members-only, or sign-in-protected content
- Downloads run fully detached from Raycast — closing Raycast or your terminal does not interrupt an in-progress download
- Auto-installs yt-dlp and ffmpeg via Homebrew if missing
